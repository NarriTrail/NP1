Hyderabad Metro services were launched in November of 2017 with the vision of creating efficient, safe, reliable and affordable public transportation system to transform how people move in the city. With an increasing ridership, the metro already looks promising as a serious mode of transport for Hyderabad. As the network of services grows, the metro is poised to witness a huge surge in ridership and demand.
